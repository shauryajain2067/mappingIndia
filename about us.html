ABOUT US
We are an inbound travel operator company providing you with comfortable, mesmerising and well-curated travel packages throughout India.
Come and experience a journey beyond worlds."MAPPING" INDIA takes you on an experience of India like never before.
Visit the Great Himalyas of the north,the Beautiful Forest of the East,
Serene Backwaters of South and Golden Desert of the West, all in one exquisite experiential travel through India, with sustainability.
Our Inspiration
We came up with this concept of a pan India tour when we were looking out for such an experience for ourselves but were not able to
find any unique combination in which we could experience all the 4 regions of India.
That was the moment when we decided to start our research and we came up with the idea of “MAPPING INDIA”.
